About ZADECLANT ENERGY

Zadeclant Energy Limited is an indigenous oil and gas services company focused on delivering specialized exploration and production (E&P) support services to upstream operators.

The company is positioned to provide technical, operational, and project support solutions across the lifecycle of oil and gas assets — from exploration through field development to production optimization.

Zadeclant integrates engineering expertise, field experience, and cost-efficient execution models to support operators in maximizing asset value while managing operational risks.
